Early Life and Career

Elon Reeve Musk is a business magnate and investor known for his ambitious goals to revolutionize transportation on Earth and in space. Born in Pretoria, South Africa in 1971, he moved to Canada at age 17 before eventually settling in the United States. His first major success came with the company Zip2, which he co-founded and sold to Compaq in 1999. He then co-founded X.com, an online financial services and e-mail payment company, which later merged to become PayPal.
